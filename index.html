❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Brain Vital Score?

The Brain Vital Score is a personalized assessment based on 12 research-inspired questions across 6 key areas: Sleep, Nutrition, Physical Activity, Stress, Social Connection, and Cognitive Training. Your score ranges from 0 to 24, giving you a snapshot of your overall brain wellness.

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. Brain Vitals works 100% offline. All features — including the assessment, daily activities, brain games, and breathing exercises — are fully available without an internet connection.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind.

How often should I retake the survey?

We recommend retaking the Brain Vital Score assessment every 2 weeks to track your progress and see how lifestyle changes are impacting your brain wellness.

How do daily activities work?

Each day, you receive 3 fresh activities from a library of 80+ brain-boosting tasks across 5 categories: Physical, Cognitive, Social, Mindfulness, and Nutrition. Complete them to build a streak and healthy habits.

Is the app really free?

Yes. Brain Vitals is completely free with no ads. There is an optional tip jar if you'd like to support continued development, but it is entirely voluntary and does not unlock any additional features.

How do I set up daily reminders?

Go to Settings (gear icon on the home screen) and scroll to "Daily Reminders." You can set custom times for your daily activities reminder, morning routine, and wind-down breathing exercise.

What brain games are available?

Brain Vitals includes 15 brain training games: Quick Math Burst, Word Chain, Number Recall, Letter Hunt, Time Estimation, Category Recall, Alphabet Reverse, Word Unscramble, Memory Pairs, Bubble Wrap, Shape Count, Sudoku, Tower of Hanoi, Peg Solitaire, and Recall Challenge.

Can I reset my data?

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can reset it by deleting and reinstalling the app. Please note this will erase all your scores, streaks, and activity history.
